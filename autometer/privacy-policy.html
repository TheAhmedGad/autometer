This Privacy Policy explains how AutoMeter handles information. The App is designed to work offline and does not require user accounts or authentication.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

The App does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on any external servers. We do not request registration, names, phone numbers, addresses, precise location, contacts, photos, or any sensitive information.

2. Data You Enter in the App

The App allows you to input car maintenance-related data such as:

  • Current mileage (odometer)
  • Maintenance items (e.g., oil change, tires)
  • Service intervals and last service mileage

This data is stored locally on your device only (local storage). It is not sent to us or to any third party.

3. How We Use Information

The App uses the data you enter only to calculate and display:

  • Next due mileage
  • Remaining kilometers
  • Maintenance status (Due / Soon / OK)

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data.

Because the App does not collect or transmit your data, there is no data sharing with advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for device-level security (e.g., screen lock). If you uninstall the App, the locally stored data may be removed by your operating system.
